ABOUT US

"One person's temporary is another person's treat."

Fashion Forever is a clothing rental subscription service based in New York City. We discovered there is an issue with wasteful production of clothing that are unused, burned, or destroyed from lack of finding its rightful usability.

Our mission is to fill and bridge the gap between Fashion and Sustainability. The fashion industry is always evolving and changing. Therefore it's a never-ending cycle, struggling to adapt to the latest trend. What about the ones left behind? Instead of trashing out, donating (high-value items), passing down to younger generations, or piling up a mountain of unworn clothes, our tactic enables users to keep a sustainable cycle running.

We strongly believe in doing social good. We give back to the community by providing directly to donation center that distributes directly to those in need such as victims of abusive relationships.

Let's help make the world a better place, together.
